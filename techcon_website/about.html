Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in San Francisco. Over the years, it has grown into a global event that connects developers, startups, and thought leaders. Each year, TechCon showcases breakthrough technologies and fosters community collaboration.

Our Mission

TechCon’s mission is to inspire innovation, encourage learning, and empower diverse voices in the technology industry. We aim to create a platform where ideas flourish and tomorrow’s tech leaders emerge.
